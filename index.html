About Me

I have a Bachelors Degree from the University of Georgia, which led me to my current role as an eCommerce Manager for a wrapping paper company. My dream role is to become a Web Developer for an athletic brand or sports company. I am married and have 2 Golden Retrievers that keep me busy! My husband and I live in Charleston, SC and love spending our free time at the beach or on our boat. I also love running and being outside.
